What is Mental Health?

Emotional Wellbeing

Mental health includes our emotional well-being—how we feel about ourselves and our ability to manage feelings and cope with difficulties.

Psychological Wellbeing

It encompasses our psychological health—our ability to think clearly, make decisions, and process information effectively.

Social Wellbeing

Mental health affects our social well-being—how we form and maintain relationships with others and function in social settings.

Good mental health improves productivity and relationships. It's not just the absence of mental illness, but the presence of positive characteristics.
